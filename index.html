Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

FinTrack ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the FinTrack – Salary & Expense Manager mobile application.

By using FinTrack, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect limited personal information required for authentication and app functionality:

  • Google account email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • User ID provided by Firebase Authentication

We do not collect or store passwords.

1.2 Financial & App Data

All financial data is entered voluntarily by the user, including:

  • Salary and income details
  • Expense records
  • Savings goals
  • Lending and borrowing records
  • Categories and descriptions
  • Receipt or bill images (optional)

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

  • Basic app usage and performance data
  • Sync status information
  • Device information required by Firebase services

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide budgeting and expense tracking features
  • Calculate daily spending limits
  • Display analytics and reports
  • Enable secure cloud synchronization
  • Improve app performance and reliability

We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Local Storage

FinTrack uses encrypted local storage on your device as the primary data store, ensuring fast performance and full offline functionality.

3.2 Cloud Storage

When enabled, data is securely backed up using Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Firestore for structured data
  • Firebase Storage for receipt images

3.3 Security Measures

  • Secure Google authentication
  • User-specific Firebase security rules
  • Encrypted local database

4. Offline Usage

FinTrack works fully offline. Data syncs to the cloud only when internet connectivity is available or when the user manually initiates synchronization.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties. Data is shared only with Google Firebase services strictly for app functionality and security.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account remains active. You may edit or delete your records within the app at any time.

7. User Control

You have full control over your data, including:

  • Editing or deleting financial records
  • Managing cloud synchronization
  • Changing currency and categories
  • Logging out securely

8. Children’s Privacy

FinTrack is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
