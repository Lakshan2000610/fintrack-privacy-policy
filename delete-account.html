Account & Data Deletion

App Name: FinTrack – Salary & Expense Manager

How to Request Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their FinTrack account and associated data by sending an email request to:

Email: fintrack.app@gmail.com

Please include the subject line: “FinTrack Account Deletion Request” and mention the email address used to sign in.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User authentication data
  • Salary and income records
  • Expense records
  • Savings goals
  • Lending and borrowing information
  • Uploaded receipt or bill images

Data Retention

After a deletion request is verified, all associated user data stored in cloud services will be permanently deleted within 7 working days.

Some minimal data may be retained temporarily if required by law or for security and fraud prevention purposes.

Offline Data

Any data stored locally on the user’s device can be removed by uninstalling the FinTrack application.

Contact

If you have questions regarding account deletion, please contact us at:
